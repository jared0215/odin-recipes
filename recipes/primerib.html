Christmas Prime Rib

The perfect Prime Rib for a succesful Christmas Dinner

A prime rib dinner is perfect for Christmas or other special occasions. This recipe includes a mustard and horseradish crust on the beef and gives you the perfect roasting time. A Prime Rib Roast is almost always our choice for a crowd-pleasing Christmas dinner. Turkey is for Thanksgiving, and we’ve never been a big ham family, so prime rib has been our default choice for years. This year, we’d like to bring you into our home finally to show you how to make the perfect prime rib roast for the holidays—or any special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 pound) boneless prime rib roast
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons coarsely ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 2 teaspoons farlic powder
  • 2 stalks celery, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 carrot, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 small unpeeled onion, quartered and separated

Au Jus Sauce:

  • 2 teaspoons concentrated beef base (paste)
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon water
